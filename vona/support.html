Frequently asked questions

How do I add an entry?

Tap the microphone and talk. Your voice is transcribed automatically, and you'll see a live preview of the words as you speak. You can also add photos, a place, and a category — or just write your entry by hand.

What does the AI do with my entries?

Each entry gets an automatic title, a short summary, and a mood. You can also chat with your journal — ask it questions about your own past entries — and read a weekly AI review of your mood and highlights.

Is my journal private?

Yes. Your entries are stored only on your phone. You can lock the app with Face ID, fingerprint, or a PIN so only you can open it.

How do I back up or move my journal to a new phone?

Go to Settings › Back up my journal to save one file with all your entries, photos, and audio to your own storage (iCloud Drive, Files, Google Drive, etc.). Use Settings › Restore a backup on your new device to bring it back.

I subscribed to Premium — how do I restore it on a new device?

Open the Premium screen and tap Restore my purchase. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

What languages does Vona support?

The app interface is available in English, French, and Spanish, and voice entries can be transcribed in any of those languages.

How do I delete an entry?

Deleted entries move to "Recently Deleted" for 30 days before being permanently removed, so you can recover them if you change your mind.
