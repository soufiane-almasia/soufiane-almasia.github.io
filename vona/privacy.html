Vona: AI Voice Journal

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile app Vona: AI Voice Journal ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. The app is operated by Soufiane Chalouh. Vona does not require you to create an account.

Information we collect

Your journal content

Your entries — text, photos, audio recordings, places, and categories — are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your journal on our own servers.

Voice transcription and AI features

When you record a voice entry, the audio is sent through our proxy to Groq's speech-to-text and language models to transcribe it and to generate titles, summaries, moods, the journal chat answers, and your weekly review. This content is processed to generate a response and is not retained by us afterwards. It is handled by Groq under its own privacy policy.

Location

If you choose to tag a place on an entry, the app uses your device's location services (and Apple/Google Maps) to find and display that place. This is only used when you actively add a place, and the result is stored locally with that entry.

Photos

Photos you add to an entry are selected from your own photo library and stored locally with your journal.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple and RevenueCat. We receive confirmation of your purchase and subscription status to unlock Premium features, but we never receive or store your payment card details.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, a local notification is scheduled on your device. No data about this is sent to us.

What we don't do

  • We don't show ads.
  • We don't use third-party analytics or advertising trackers.
  • We don't sell or share your journal content with anyone.

Backups and exports

Backing up or exporting your journal is something you choose to do. When you back up or export, the app creates a file that you save to a destination you pick (such as iCloud Drive, Google Drive, or Files) — it is not sent to us.

Third-party services

The app relies on the following service providers, each of which processes data under its own privacy policy:

  • Groq (voice transcription & AI text generation) — groq.com/privacy-policy
  • RevenueCat (subscription management) — revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple (in-app purchases, App Store, Maps, Face ID) — apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Google (Maps, on Android) — policies.google.com/privacy

Your choices

  • App lock: Protect your journal with Face ID, fingerprint, or a PIN in Settings.
  • Location: You can deny or revoke location access at any time in your device settings; you simply won't be able to tag places on entries.
  • Notifications: Daily reminders can be turned on or off at any time in Settings.
  • Deletion: Deleting an entry or the app removes it from your device. Since we don't hold a copy of your journal on our servers, there is nothing further for us to delete on our end.

Children's privacy

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact
