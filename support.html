Frequently asked questions

How do I play?

Swipe across the letter wheel to spell a word, then release. Each clue is either a photo (a flag, monument, planet, or animal) or a short trivia question. Fill every word to complete the grid.

How do hints and coins work?

If you're stuck, spend coins to reveal a single letter or a whole word. Earn coins by completing puzzles and claiming your daily reward.

I bought "Remove Ads" — how do I restore it on a new device?

Open the shop or settings and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Does the game work offline?

Yes. Puzzles are bundled with the app, so you can play anywhere without an internet connection. Ads and some rewards may require a connection.

What is the daily puzzle?

A fresh grid unlocks every day. Play daily to build your streak and earn bonus rewards.

How do I report a wrong answer or clue?

Email us the level and the word, and we'll fix it in an update. Thank you for helping improve the game!
